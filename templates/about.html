About SpotSalon

The Spot Saloon Services Management system is designed to bridge the gap between customers seeking salon services and salon business owners who wish to promote their offerings online. The primary goal of this web-based platform is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly interface where customers can discover and connect with salons in their region, while salon owners can list their services and manage their online presence.
